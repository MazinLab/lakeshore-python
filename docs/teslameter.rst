F41 & F71 Teslameters
=====================
The Lake Shore single-axis (F41) and multi-axis (F71) Teslameters provide highly accurate field strength measurements. More information about the instrument can be found `on our website`_.

.. _on our website: https://www.lakeshore.com/products/Gaussmeters/F71-F41-teslameters/Pages/Overview.aspx
